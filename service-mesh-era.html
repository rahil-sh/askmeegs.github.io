The Service Mesh Era was a six-part Google Cloud Blog series about the why, what, and how of Service Mesh tools.

The series was designed to introduce the Istio service mesh project, and also accompanied the launch of Anthos Service Mesh, a Google Cloud service mesh product that implements Istio's APIs. Within this series, I authored three posts and implemented the demos.
